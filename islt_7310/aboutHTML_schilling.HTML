The Basics of Tort Law


Welcome to Introduction to Torts. Torts is an area of law and unfortunately is not as sweet as tarts. However, my goal is to help make this a sweet learning experience. In the end, you will find that there are almost as many flavors of tarts as there are of areas of tort law.


I have been a lawyer for over 20 years. I received my Juris Doctor (law degree) from Creighton University in 1987. Over the years, I have taught law at the community college level. Additionally, I have trained new lawyers, interns and paralegals about torts and the practice of law. I've been told that I am a good teacher. I hope that this course will enhance your ability to legally analyze situations and appreciate tort law.
